About PITON LLC

Production and Technological Support of Petrochemistry — engineering axial fan systems for Russia's most critical industrial infrastructure since 1992.

Who We Are

Engineering Excellence Since 1992

PITON LLC (ООО «ПИТОН») specializes in the design, manufacture, and supply of high-performance axial fan systems for cooling towers and air-cooled heat exchangers. Based in Moscow, we serve Russia's largest industrial enterprises — from oil refineries and petrochemical complexes to metallurgical plants and power stations.

Our name stands for «Производственно-Технологическое Обеспечение Нефтехимии» — Production and Technological Support of Petrochemistry. This mission defines everything we do: delivering engineering solutions that keep petrochemical and industrial cooling systems running at peak efficiency, 24/7, 365 days a year.

Over three decades, we have equipped more than 40 major enterprises with our fan systems, impellers, diffusers, and electric motors — accumulating millions of operational hours without a single critical failure attributed to our equipment.

Our History

Three Decades of Industrial Innovation

1992

Company Founded

PITON LLC established in Moscow with a focus on fan systems for the petrochemical industry. First contracts with Russian oil refineries for cooling tower fan replacements.

Late 1990s

Composite Blade Technology

Developed proprietary fiberglass-reinforced polymer blade manufacturing process, eliminating corrosion issues plaguing metal blades in cooling tower environments. Introduced stainless steel leading-edge protection.

2000s

Enterprise Client Base Expansion

Secured contracts with Gazpromneft, Rosneft, Lukoil, and Tatneft — becoming a trusted supplier to Russia's largest oil and gas companies. Expanded product line to include complete fan systems with matched low-speed motors.

2010s

Full Product Range & International Markets

Launched the complete VG Series (2.5m to 10.4m), AVO Series for air-cooled heat exchangers, and D Series composite diffusers. Expanded to Kazakhstan, Belarus, Bulgaria, and Uzbekistan. Achieved 40+ enterprise client milestone.

Present

30+ Years of Proven Performance

PITON continues to serve Russia's critical industrial infrastructure with the broadest range of cooling tower fan systems in the domestic market — from 1.6-meter AVO fans to the massive 10.4-meter VG-104 for large-scale industrial cooling towers.

What We Do

Our Capabilities

Custom Engineering

Fan system configuration optimized for specific tower geometry, airflow requirements, and site conditions. Every installation is a precision-engineered solution.

Composite Manufacturing

In-house production of fiberglass-reinforced polymer blades and diffuser sectors using proprietary layup processes and quality-controlled materials.

Motor Matching

Complete drive system specification — purpose-built low-speed motors matched to fan requirements, eliminating gearboxes and reducing maintenance.

Supply Chain Integration

Direct supply to procurement departments at major enterprises. Established logistics for oversized and heavy cargo to remote industrial sites.

Technical Support

Engineering consultation for blade angle optimization, vibration analysis, retrofit planning, and fan system modernization of existing installations.

International Delivery

Export capabilities to CIS countries — Kazakhstan, Belarus, Uzbekistan — and international markets including Bulgaria. Full export documentation support.
