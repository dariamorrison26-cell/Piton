Key Features

Composite Blades

Fiberglass-reinforced polymer construction with stainless steel leading-edge protection for maximum durability in industrial environments.

Unlimited Blade Angle

Full range blade angle adjustment for precise airflow optimization. No mechanical stops — configure to exact site requirements.

Low-Speed Motors

Purpose-built ВАСО, АСВО, and ВАСВ series motors designed for quiet, continuous-duty cooling tower service. No gearbox required.

TU Certified

Manufactured to TU3689-001-00220311-2015 standard. Full quality traceability from raw material to delivered assembly.

Modular Assembly

Sectioned blade and hub assembly enables transport to remote installation sites. Field-installable with standard industrial tooling.

Applications

VG Series fans are installed in induced-draft cooling towers at:

  • Metallurgical plants and steel mills

  • Large-scale HVAC and district cooling systems
