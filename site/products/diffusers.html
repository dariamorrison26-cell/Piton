Complete D Series Specifications

D Series diffusers are manufactured from fiberglass composite for corrosion resistance, light weight, and aerodynamic precision. Sectored construction enables shipment by standard transport vehicles and rapid on-site assembly. Available in both standard (SK) and deep-profile (SDM/SI) configurations.

Construction Advantages

Sectored Construction

Each diffuser is manufactured in modular sectors that can be transported by standard vehicles and assembled on-site, eliminating the need for oversized load transport.

Corrosion Resistant

Fiberglass composite is inherently corrosion-resistant, making D Series diffusers ideal for the high-moisture, chemically aggressive environment inside cooling towers.

Aerodynamic Profile

Optimized diffuser geometry reduces exit losses and improves fan efficiency. Deep-profile variants (SI, SDM) deliver additional airflow recovery for demanding applications.
