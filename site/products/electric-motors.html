Key Advantages

Direct Drive — No Gearbox

Motors operate at exact fan RPM, eliminating gearbox losses, maintenance, oil changes, and the most common failure point in cooling tower fan systems.

Low Noise

Low-speed operation at 111–375 RPM produces significantly less noise than standard high-speed motors with gearbox reducers. Suitable for noise-sensitive locations.

Continuous-Duty Rated

Designed for 24/7 continuous operation in the harsh cooling tower environment — heat, humidity, water spray, and chemical exposure. IP55 protection standard.

Vertical Shaft Mounting

Purpose-designed for vertical installation on cooling tower fan decks, with thrust bearings rated for full impeller weight plus aerodynamic loads.

Matched to VG Series

Each motor model is precisely specified for the corresponding VG fan model — power, speed, shaft, and mounting are pre-matched for drop-in installation.

High-Voltage Option

The ВАСВ series at 6,000V enables the 200 kW VG-104 fan to operate with lower current draw, reducing cable sizes and distribution losses for remote tower installations.
