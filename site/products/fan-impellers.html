Complete RK Series Specifications

RK Series impellers are available as complete replacement assemblies for all major cooling tower types. Each impeller features fiberglass-reinforced polymer blades with unlimited angle adjustment, mounted on precision-machined steel hubs. Available in 4-blade and 6-blade configurations.

Blade Construction

Fiberglass-Reinforced Polymer

Multi-layer composite construction provides exceptional strength-to-weight ratio, corrosion resistance, and fatigue life exceeding 20 years.

Stainless Steel Leading Edge

Stainless steel cladding on blade leading edges protects against erosion from water droplets, dust, and debris in cooling tower environments.

Unlimited Angle Adjustment

No mechanical stops — blades can be set to any angle, enabling precise optimization of airflow for specific tower geometry and operating conditions.

Replacement & Retrofit

RK Series impellers are engineered as direct replacements for standard cooling tower fan impellers. Our engineering team can specify the correct model for your existing fan assembly based on:

  • Existing fan model and manufacturer
  • Cooling tower type and dimensions
  • Required airflow rate and pressure
  • Motor specifications and shaft configuration
  • Site altitude and ambient conditions
